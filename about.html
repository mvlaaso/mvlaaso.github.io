MVLA-Adult School Orchestra: About

South Bay Community Orchestra


The South Bay Community Orchestra was founded in 1972 under the name of "The Melotones" by two retired US Navy Officers, Capt. John Dorsett and his wife Louise, a Navy nurse. Their hard work and devotion to the principle that this should be an enjoyable, unpressured opportunity for musicians to renew old skills or build new ones, has brought rewards to the participants and multiple audiences for over twenty years. Though primarily seniors, the orchestra is open to anyone with an instrument, ability to read music and time to rehearse on Wednesday afternoons from 1 to 3. From the five original members, there are now fifty-five registered musicians. Concerts are usually staffed with forty musicians and present a wide repertoire from Pops to Classical. The group is under the auspices of Mountain View Los Altos Adult Education and rehearses at the Mountain View Senior Center on Escuela Avenue in Mountain View.
